  Wave Energy Prize provides an opportunity for participants to:  <ul>   <li>Win a

  of a 1/20<sup>th</sup> scale model WEC device for testing.</li>   <li>Participate

  and Seakeeping (MASK) Basin in Carderock, Md., the nation’s premier wave testing
  facility, at no cost to the finalist teams.</li>   <li>Benefit from many opportunities
